TERM 1 - WEEK 7 2026
 
Message from the Principal
Dr Sean Mangan
Principal
St Patrick’s Parish
School
Dear Parents and Carers,

It has certainly been a busy and rewarding fortnight here at St Patrick’s. Our recent school photo day ran very smoothly. Thank you to all our families for ensuring students arrived looking so neat and well-presented. The pride our students show in their school is always wonderful to see, and these photos will be a lovely record of the 2026 school year.

Our scheduled fire drill had to be postponed due to poor weather conditions. Student safety drills are an important part of our school procedures, and we will reschedule this in the coming weeks.

Congratulations to all the students who represented St Patrick’s at the Archdiocesan Swimming Championships in Queanbeyan.

Last week also marked the beginning of NAPLAN testing for our students. Like many schools across the country, we experienced some challenges with the online testing system. Please be assured that these issues were widespread and that no child will be disadvantaged as a result.
